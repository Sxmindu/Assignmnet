Hometown

Battaramulla is a suburb of Colombo, situated 8.4 km from the city centre at Colombo Fort. It is one of the fastest developing administrative, commercial and residential areas in the Colombo District, being home to the country's elite. 

Places to Visit

Jana Kala Kendraya

You can do some window-shopping at Jana Kala Kendraya during your trip to Battaramulla. Amble around this culturally rich area's seaside and enjoy its top-notch restaurants.

Diyatha Uyana

Explore the great outdoors at Diyatha Uyana, a lovely green space in Battaramulla. Stroll along the seaside in this culturally rich area, or check out its top-notch restaurants.

Diyasaru Park

Explore the great outdoors at Diyasaru Park, a lovely green space in Battaramulla. Amble around the area's parks or visit its shops.
